About Us

Celebrate7 is a platform to discover and promote cultural, social, and charity events across the North Eastern states of India. Our goal is to provide a clean and user-friendly interface where both locals and others can find and participate in events happening in the region.

We believe in building community and supporting organizers by helping them reach a wider audience. Whether it’s a festival, online seminar, or a local charity drive — Celebrate7 makes it visible and accessible.
